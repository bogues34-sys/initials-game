How to play

You're shown the initials of a real athlete. Guess who it is!

  • Up to 3 outs per inning — wrong guesses are outs
  • Reveal hints to help, but each hint lowers your max hit:

HR No hints used
3B 1 hint revealed
2B 2 hints revealed
1B All 3 hints revealed

Runners on base score runs as you get hits — just like real baseball!

Difficulty

  • Rookie — All-time legends you definitely know
  • Pro — All-stars and franchise players
  • Legend — Deep cuts who met the games-played threshold

Daily puzzle — Everyone gets the same athlete each day. One attempt, no hints deducted from the first guess. Share your result!

Free play — Unlimited innings, pick your sport and difficulty.
